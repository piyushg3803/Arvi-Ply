Description

Arvi MR/BWR plywood is ideal for robust interior construction work and many others application which required moisture resistance. Arvi MR/BWR Plywood consists of Hardwood species along with thick Gurjan Face. MUF resin has been used to get excellent properties of moisture resistance. Arvi MR/BWR Marine plywood is pretreated with world best glue line poisoned and post treated with eco-friendly preservative chemicals. It contains preservative components that prevents damaged caused by termite and borer. Arvi MR/BWR plywood has low emission formal dehydrate compliance with E1 and comes withlS 303 certifications.
